Call for papers

15 December 2025

Net Zero Carbon Built Environment 2

Submission deadline: 01 December 2026

The intersection of climate resilience, societal transitions, and emerging technologies in the built environment has become a timely and globally relevant topic. Future built environments will face increasingly demanding challenges, requiring integrative and holistic approaches to all aspects affecting energy performance, comfort, and health. This Special Issue provides a platform for recent research and innovations aimed at achieving a net-zero carbon built environment, offering insights that support both scientific advancement and practical implementation.

Scope:

1. Climate, Environment, and Society
  • Climate impacts: rising temperatures, wildfires, floods
  • Resilient cities and infrastructure
  • Regenerative landscapes and urban planning
  • Low-carbon and energy-positive communities
  • Health, wellbeing, and comfort
  • Social equity, justice, and inclusive transitions
  • Citizen science and participatory approaches
  • Sustainable and inclusive mobility
2. Technology and Innovation
  • Artificial intelligence in the built environment
  • Digital transformation and smart design tools
  • Renewable and low-carbon energy technologies
  • Energy storage, hybrid systems, and integration
  • Performance monitoring, digital twins, and operational outcomes
  • Smart grids, energy flexibility, and management platforms
