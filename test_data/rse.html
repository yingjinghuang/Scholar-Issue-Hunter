Geospatial Foundation Models for Advancing Remote Sensing of Environment

Submission deadline: 30 June 2026

AI-driven geospatial foundation models mark a paradigm shift in remote sensing. This special issue aims to serve as a landmark collection highlighting the convergence of AI, remote sensing, and environmental monitoring. It showcases cutting-edge methods for detecting, attributing, and forecasting natural and social environmental change; evaluates the generalization, interpretability, and transferability of foundation models across diverse sensors and landscapes; promotes open and reproducible, cloud-native Earth observation workflows; and fosters collaboration among academia, industry, and government to translate AI-powered geospatial intelligence into actionable sustainability outcomes.
